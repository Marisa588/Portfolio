About Me

Hello! My name is Marisa Charlier and I am currently a student at 1150 Academy for Web Development. Before 1150, I attended Purdue University and earned a Bachelors Degree in Organizational Leadership and Supervision. I also earned a certificate in Entrepenuership.

After graduating Purdue I had a few different jobs ranging from marketing to retail. My most recent position was Customer Service for Motionwear. What I loved most about what I did was creating fun uniforms that teams were excited to show off during competitions. In 2020 Motionwear closed, and then COVID hit. I was able to reflect on what exactly I wanted my next move to be.

After a lot of research I concluded that Web Development would be a great career move. I would still be able to create awesome projects while being challenged everyday with the always changing technology trends. So far this career shift has been a great fit and I'm so excited to see where it takes me!
